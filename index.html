What is computer Science?? 🤔

Computer science is the study of how to use computers to solve problems. It involves developing software, designing computer systems and networks, and understanding how computers can be used to process and analyze data. In short, computer science is all about using computers to make our lives easier and to solve complex problems.

Out of the available computer science topics, i have picked software development as my area of interest. Software development is the process of creating computer programs, which are sets of instructions that tell a computer what to do. This involves designing the program, writing the code, and testing it to make sure it works as intended.

Software development branches into fields as well. A programmer can choose to create sofware for anything that has a screen! Including but not limited to: Smart TVs, smart watches, computers, phones, even fridges In this website, i will be talking about web development; which is the art of crating websites and making them work.

This website will showcase my journey in learning web development, and some of the resources i have used to achieving the latter. Let's Gooooo 🚀
